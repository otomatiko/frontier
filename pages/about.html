Our Mission

Frontier's Mission is to help the scientific community to spread awareness about our solar system and its neighboring stars and the constellation, by giving the mainstream media an interactive way to learn about the cosmos and beyond. also, Frontier's mission is to help humanity by researching and improving space observation apparatus and technical equipments that will help the future space missions and explorations.

Our Vision

Frontier aims to encourage people to participate on solving our planets problem, the global warming. and to help the existing research institute to make humanity a multi-planetary specie.
